# Myntra Smart Stylist Project Alignment

## Project Overview
This project simulates a Storefront Product initiative focused on discovery quality, personalization, and funnel improvement.  
The app converts natural language intent into ranked fashion recommendations, explains why each recommendation is relevant, and tracks KPI movement in real time.

## How AI Is Used
### 1. Intent Interpretation
- User enters plain language requests such as monsoon brunch in Bangalore or gym wear for office commute.
- AI style matching logic extracts intent signals and maps them to style attributes in the product dataset.
- Output is ranked top picks with intent confidence scoring.

### 2. Recommendation Reasoning
- Every recommendation includes a Why this works for you explanation.
- The explanation translates style logic into user friendly business value.
- This supports trust, reduces confusion, and improves decision speed.

### 3. Value Intelligence Layer
- The app compares simulated market price references with Myntra price.
- It generates Best Value on Myntra narratives such as lower than comparable style or exclusive inventory.
- This directly supports reduced platform leakage by strengthening in app confidence.

## How Other Product Systems Are Used
### Analytics and Problem Solving
- Live KPI monitor tracks:
  - Search Conversion Rate
  - Average Intent Match Score
  - Null Search Reduction or retention proxy depending on build mode
- Metrics are session driven and tied to user actions such as discovery runs and shortlist actions.
- This supports hypothesis validation with measurable evidence.

### Product Operations
- SOP section defines catalog tagging, pricing feed hygiene, and data quality checks.
- Documentation outlines repeatable process for category and pricing teams.
- This aligns with process optimization and internal knowledge base responsibilities.

### Program Management
- Roadmap and launch checklist content maps Engineering, Design, and QA handoffs.
- Includes readiness framing for release quality and cross functional coordination.
- Demonstrates launch planning mindset beyond just feature coding.
